[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Tangled up in the fading light,
Your whiskey kiss on a summer night.
We have nowhere to be but right here,
Whispering secrets into the clear.

[Verse 1]
The gravel road is dust and dreams,
Lit up by the dashboard gleams.
You slide across the leather seat,
And suddenly the world is complete.

[Pre-Chorus]
I can feel the heat rising in the dark,
Every little touch is a dangerous spark.
Pull you in close, let the shadows fall,
There is nothing but the magic of it all.

[Chorus]
Oh, these endless summer nights,
Bathed in the glow of the firefly lights.
Losing my breath when you look my way,
Wishing the morning would drift away.

[Post-Chorus]
Yeah, drift away, just you and me,
Lost in the warmth of the memory.

[Verse 2]
The radio plays a slow, sweet tune,
Dancing along with the silver moon.
Your hands trace the lines upon my chest,
Of all the moments, I love this best.

[Pre-Chorus 2]
I can feel the heat rising in the dark,
Every little touch is a dangerous spark.
Pull you in close, let the shadows fall,
There is nothing but the magic of it all.

[Chorus]
Oh, these endless summer nights,
Bathed in the glow of the firefly lights.
Losing my breath when you look my way,
Wishing the morning would drift away.

[Post-Chorus 2]
Yeah, drift away, just you and me,
Lost in the warmth of the memory.

[Bridge]
Tangled up in the fading light,
Your whiskey kiss on a summer night.
You are the only heaven that I really need,
Bringing out the wild and the tender in Saeed.
Letting the shadows hold us in close,
You are the sweet addiction I crave the most.

[Spoken]
[Spoken] (I just want to hold you right here.)
[Spoken] (Do not let the sunrise take you away.)

[Final Chorus]
Oh, these endless summer nights,
Bathed in the glow of the firefly lights.
Losing my breath when you look my way,
Wishing the morning would drift away.
[Whispered] (Drift away)
